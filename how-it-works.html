How It Works

Title commitment in. Risk intelligence out.

Upload a PDF. Our five-domain AI pipeline reads every exception, classifies every encumbrance, and delivers a ranked risk report — in the time it takes to get a coffee.

The Pipeline

Five domains. One pass. Every risk scored.

1

Encumbrance Register

Reads Schedule B-II. Extracts every exception. Maps each to one of 40 canonical types.

2

Spatial Risk

Flags what each encumbrance does to the dirt: buildable area, access, infrastructure, setbacks.

3

Lifecycle Impact

Scores every encumbrance across four stages: Acquisition DD, Zoning, Engineering, Construction.

4

Acquisition Decision

Per-encumbrance go/no-go: affects pricing? Extend diligence? Kill the deal?

5

MEIS Report

Ranked material exceptions. GREEN / YELLOW / RED risk signal. Action items prioritized.

What You Get
